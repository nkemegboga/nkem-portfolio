About Me

Petroleum Engineering graduate (BS & MS) from the University of Texas at Austin (UT). My experience in petroleum engineering, particularly in reservoir engineering and simulation includes internships at Statoil, Reveal Energy Services, the Texas Commission on Environmental Quality, and three years as a researcher and teaching assistant at UT Austin, where I explored novel mechanisms for improving shale oil recovery.

I am currently exploring new opportunities as a Galvanize Data Science fellow (Python, MATLAB, SQL, Pandas, Numpy, scikitlearn, Machine Learning), as well as taking classes in web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery) and hosting a weekly soccer podcast.
